Magic Forest is a fictional world where you will discover many magical creatures and plants such as talking trees, elves, giant pumpkin monsters, and more. In this magical forest, you can explore every corner, discover its secrets and surprises. The world is full of adventure and exploration, and it is a place worth visiting.

- Scene Information -
Setting
A fictional world filled with magical and fantastical elements, detached from the constraints of the real world, brimming with magic and mysterious forces.
Location
Situated in a distant place, surrounded by towering mountains and dense foliage. Within the forest, there is a colossal pumpkin sculpture, and every corner is filled with surprises and adventures.
Time
Time is an eternal night, perpetually shrouded in a mysterious ambiance. Here, moonlight seeps through the gaps between leaves, illuminating beautiful flowers and enchanting creatures. Twinkling stars and silvery moonlight create a dreamlike atmosphere.

AKUMA NO KO
- Story Introduction -

In an ancient village, there was a boy who was shunned by the people around him because he had a horn growing on his head. The boy believed himself to be a monster and lived in hiding, barely surviving in this world. Until one day, in a narrow alley, the boy met a girl who harbored no prejudice against him, and he slowly opened his heart to her.

However, their happiness didn't last long. One day, rumors began to circulate in the village about the need to hunt down witches, and due to her close association with the boy, the kind-hearted girl was accused of being a hypocritical witch. The boy, witnessing the only pillar of support in his world being killed, succumbed to a mixture of grief and anger, tapping into a dark power. Overnight, he annihilated everyone in the village. Yet, even in doing so, he couldn't bring back the girl.
